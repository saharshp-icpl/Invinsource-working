NDA

Please accept Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA).

A Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is a legal contract between two or more parties that outlines confidential material, knowledge, or information that the parties wish to share with each other for certain purposes but want to keep confidential. The NDA aims to protect the confidential information and prevent its unauthorized use or disclosure by the recipient.

The NDA can be unilateral, where only one party discloses information, or mutual, where both parties exchange confidential information. The agreement typically defines the scope of the confidential information, the duration of the agreement, and the consequences of breaching the agreement.
